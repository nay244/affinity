About Us

Focused on innovation and quality, Affinity designs personal peripherals to help people enjoy a better experience with the digital world.
We started in 1981 with mice, which (new at the time) provided a more intuitive way of interacting with a personal computer.
We became the worldwide leader in computer mice, and have reinvented the mouse in dozens of ways to match the evolving needs of PC and laptop users.
